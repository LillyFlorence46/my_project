Day 1: Arrival and Eiffel Tower

Welcome to Paris! Begin your adventure with a visit to the iconic Eiffel Tower. Spend your afternoon exploring the area and enjoying the stunning views from the top.

Day 2: Louvre Museum and Notre-Dame Cathedral

Visit the world-famous Louvre Museum to see masterpieces such as the Mona Lisa. In the afternoon, take a walk to the Notre-Dame Cathedral and explore its Gothic architecture.

Day 3: Montmartre and Sacré-Cœur

Spend your day wandering the charming streets of Montmartre, visiting art galleries, and enjoying the bohemian atmosphere. End your day at the Sacré-Cœur Basilica for a panoramic view of Paris.

Day 4: Seine River Cruise and Shopping

Take a relaxing cruise on the Seine River to see Paris from a different perspective. Spend the afternoon shopping on the Champs-Élysées and exploring the boutiques.

Travel Tips

  • Buy tickets in advance for popular attractions to avoid long lines.
  • Wear comfortable shoes for walking around the city.
  • Try local French cuisine and pastries at local cafés and bakeries.
